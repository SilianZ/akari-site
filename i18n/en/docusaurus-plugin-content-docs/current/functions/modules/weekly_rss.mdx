# weekly_rss

-   [开源]\(help/faq#Why some modules are not open sources)

This module can subscribe to the weekly pages of the Chinese Minecraft Wiki and Tea Hotels.

## `weekly_rss`

-   [订阅]\(/docs/help/reading#command type)

When enabled, you will subscribe to the Chinese Minecraft Wiki weekly page (updated on 1 8:30 per week).

## `taahouse_weekly_rss`

-   [订阅]\(/docs/help/reading#command type)

Once enabled, you will subscribe to the weekly page of the Tea Museum weekly (updated on one 8:30 per week).
